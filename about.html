About Us

CYON IJEDE DEANERY
A Thriving Community of Faith and Growth

CYON Ijede Deanery is a vibrant and closely-knit community of over 500 passionate youths from 15 parishes, united in faith and service. Through empowering programs, spiritual retreats, leadership training, and dynamic events, we nurture young Catholics to grow in faith, leadership, and social responsibility. From sports tournaments to cultural celebrations, charity outreaches to inspiring conferences, CYON Ijede Deanery continues to shape the future of Catholic youth, fostering a strong sense of purpose and belonging. Join us as we make a lasting impact!

DEANERY GENERAL MEETINGS

CYON Ijede Deanery rotates its General Meetings amongst the 15 Parishes
The meetings are held Bi-Monthly as seen on the fliers

Each Parish is to be represented by at least three (3) delegates at General Meetings, the President and Secretary must be part of these delegates.

Our Vision

We are committed to promoting our core values of:

  • Moral Integrity
  • Profession and Vocation
  • Social Communication
  • Evangelization
  • The Youth and Socialization
  • Empowerment
  • Leadership Development
  • Youth Spirituality

Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite a generation of passionate, faith-driven young people who are empowered to lead, serve, and transform their communities. Rooted in the principles of faith, service, and leadership, we strive to nurture a culture of purpose, resilience, and positive impact, equipping youths to be beacons of hope and change in the world.
