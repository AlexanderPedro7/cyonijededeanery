Deanery Praise Night: Exponential Praise

Deanery Praise Night: Exponential Praise is a youth vigil and praise night dedicated to creating an atmosphere of worship, renewal, and spiritual upliftment. This gathering offers young people an opportunity to come together in praise, prayer, and fellowship, deepening their faith and strengthening their connection with God. Through powerful worship sessions, inspiring messages, and moments of reflection.

Deanery Youth Convention

The Deanery Youth Convention is a vibrant gathering designed to inspire, empower, and unite young people in faith and fellowship. Rooted in the teachings of Christ, this convention provides an opportunity for youth to deepen their spiritual lives, engage in meaningful discussions, and strengthen their sense of community. Through uplifting worship, inspiring talks, interactive sessions, and moments of prayer, participants will be encouraged to live out their faith boldly and become agents of positive change in their churches and society.

Deanery Youth Symposium

The Deanery Youth Symposium is a dynamic gathering designed to inspire, educate, and empower young people to embrace their faith and purpose in today’s world. Under the theme "The Awakening," this symposium calls on the youth to rise to their spiritual, moral, and social responsibilities with renewed passion and commitment. Topics: Youth, Leadership & Youth Policy | Financial Intelligence

Deanery Youth Essay Competition

The Deanery Youth Essay Competition invites young people to express their thoughts on faith, leadership, and personal growth. This competition encourages intellectual engagement and creative reflection on themes that impact their lives and communities. Participants will have the chance to showcase their writing skills, share their perspectives, and inspire positive change within the Church and society as there are also prices to be won.

Leadership Seminar

Leadership begins with action. As emerging leaders, you have the power to shape the future of our youth community by living out the values of faith, service, and unity. During this seminar, take the initiative—engage in meaningful discussions, share your vision, and commit to a project that will make a lasting impact. Step up, lead with purpose, and be the change our community needs.
